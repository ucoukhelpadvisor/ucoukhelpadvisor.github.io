Why Account Security Matters

Step 1: Complete Activation Properly

Visit U.co.uk/activate in your browser. Sign in if you already have an account, or register if you are new. Enter the activation code exactly as provided to unlock full account functionality and security tools.

Step 2: Use a Strong, Unique Password

  • Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid using names or birthdates.
  • Never reuse passwords across different sites.

Step 3: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

After activating your account, go to your account settings to enable 2FA. Choose SMS verification or an authenticator app. This keeps your account secure even if someone guesses your password.

Step 4: Monitor Account Activity

  • Check recent logins and connected devices.
  • Look out for logins from unknown locations.
  • Report anything suspicious to U.co.uk support.

Step 5: Be Cautious of Phishing

  • Only access your account using U.co.uk/activate.
  • Don’t click on links in suspicious emails or texts.
  • Never share your password or activation code with anyone.

Step 6: Secure Your Devices

  • Install antivirus and anti-malware software.
  • Keep your apps and OS up to date.
  • Avoid using public Wi-Fi for account access.

Step 7: Keep Recovery Options Updated

  • Ensure your backup email and phone number are correct.
  • Update them if you change contact information.

Step 8: Log Out on Shared Devices

Always log out when using a shared or public computer. This protects your personal info and prevents unauthorized access.
